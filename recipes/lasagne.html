This is the recipe for Lasagna

This is my mom's tasty special homemade lasagna. It's best served with some garlic bread!

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor!

You don't have to be a chef to make it. You can try it yourself at home today!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
