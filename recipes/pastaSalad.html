Pasta Salad

This pasta salad is nice and colorful, easy to make, and great for summer picnics or parties.

This easy pasta salad recipe comes together quickly with convenient ingredients and colorful vegetables. The recipe was given to me by a dear friend many years ago, and I've been making it ever since!

Making this salad will take 20 minutes to prep and 10 minutes to cook. It serves 6 portions.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning Mix
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
